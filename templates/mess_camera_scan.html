{# ── How It Works ── #}
{{ card_heading("HOW IT WORKS", "Open source, no app install") }}
  1. Student opens their pass on their phone (bookmarked link, no app needed)
  2. Pass shows a QR code that rotates monthly — HMAC-SHA256 signed, can't be forged or shared
  3. This scanner reads it using the phone camera via the html5-qrcode library (open source, MIT license)
  4. Server validates the token cryptographically and records the entry

Algorithm: HMAC-SHA256(secret, "studentID:roll:period") truncated to 12 hex chars. Period = YYYY-MM (monthly) or ODD/EVEN-YYYY (semester). Old tokens auto-expire. QR generation: QRCode.js (MIT). QR scanning: html5-qrcode (Apache 2.0).
