{# ── GST Tax Codes Info ── #}
{{ card_heading("GST TAX CODES", "Auto-applied based on vendor GSTIN") }}

CATALYST detects whether a purchase is intra-state or inter-state from the first 2 digits of vendor GSTIN vs your company GSTIN. It auto-splits into CGST+SGST (same state) or IGST (different state) at 18% default rate. Tally's GST module will reconcile these against your GSTR-2B during return filing.
